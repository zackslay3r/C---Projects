Completing the Transaction

To complete a transaction when you are on the Orders page, simply click the Submit button on the orders page.

Your items will ship at the earliest possible time once our staff have processed your order. We will email you to inform you of your game being shipped.

Items typically take 2 - 5 working days to reach most Australian locations.

If your items do not arrive on time, or arrive in poor condition, please contact us at 02 6226 1234.
