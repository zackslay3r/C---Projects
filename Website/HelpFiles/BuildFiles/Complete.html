Completing the Transaction

To complete a transaction when you are on the Orders page, click the Submit button on the order page.

Your game/games will be shipped at the earliest possible time once our staff team have dealt with your order. We will email you to inform you of your game being shipped.

Items typically take 2 - 5 working days to reach most Australian locations.

If your items do not arrive on time, or arrive in poor condition, please contact us at 02 6226 1234.
